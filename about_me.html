About Me

Otherwise, I am a big video game enthusiast, I play music (can even read sheet music!), I draw pixel art, cook food, do math, and- oh, too specific? Anyways, I have created this website to show my skills. Normally, I would either be playing video games, studying more code or do lots and lots of pixel art. Games like Final Fantasy, then those SRPGs like Fire Emblem is what got me into the game's storyline. Since then, I've been enjoying storylines that involves tragic change of events and... well... feels like watching a soap opera, if I were to be honest. 🤣
